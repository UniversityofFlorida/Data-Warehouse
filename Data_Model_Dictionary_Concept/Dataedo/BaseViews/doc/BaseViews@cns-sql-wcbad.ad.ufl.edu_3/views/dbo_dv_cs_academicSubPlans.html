/*****************************************************************************************

  Name:		    dv_cs_academicSubPlans

  Description:  Academic SubPlans - Campus Solutions data
  
  				A type 2 (Slow-Changing) dimension.
				Academic Sub Plans are areas of further specialization within academic plans,
				and	are tied to Academic Plans. An Academic Sub Plan can be defined as a minor, 
				a concentration, or a specialization.
				The effectiveStartDate and effectiveEndDate columns indicate the time frame
				during which the Academic Sub Plan was effective.
				The currentIndicator can be used to filter which Academic Sub Plans are currently active.
				 
				The join between this dimension and the fact table should be with subplanSID.

  Modified:     
                
*****************************************************************************************/
